About us

We are a small, mobile detailing business located in Rexburg, ID with experience in the field. We have obtained experience through careful practice and guidance from many other professionals in the field. We believe that our cars are like family, and so many memories are made with them from our first car to our dream car. We know how valuable each car can be to you. As a business, we treat your car as our number one priority and give you the care and shine your car deserves. We look forward to taking care of you and all your car's needs!

LOCATION

We are mobile company to give our customers more of peace of mind. We come to you to care for your vehicle. We come to: - Blackfoot/Shelley - Idaho Falls -Rigby -St. Anthony -Driggs -Victor If you are not located in Rexburg, we might charge a travel fee depending on the distance. We are located at 146 N 1st W Building 4, Rexburg, ID. It is our little heated garage if you prefer to drop off your car. We will be detailing any of your cars here during the winter season. If you have a heated garage we can come to you as well!
